Not Your Typical Review System

For each restaurant, you'll see standard metrics like the overall star rating, recommendations, and culinary information, but you'll also have access to more subjective metrics like a portion size, price, and deliciousness rating. And it's all powered by you. You leave the reviews and help Yalies find the best places to eat.

The Bang for Buck™ Rating

The Bang for Buck™ rating weighs the ratings for portion size, price, and deliciousness to give you the perfect idea of how much bang you're getting for your buck. We want you maximizing your money and your experience. That's really important.
